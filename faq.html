Frequently Asked Questions

Where is MoSuke located?

MoSuke is located at 11 Rue Raymond Losserand, 75014 Paris, across the street from Hotel Mistral

Do you require reservations?

Reservations are required for all parties at this time and can be booked dirctly online through Tock

Are you able to accomodate guests with dietary restrictions?

Due to the quality and availability of our ingredients, dietary restrictions presented unexpectedly at the table may not be accomodated. We require 72 hours advance notice for all restrictions. When booking a reservation through Tock, please make note of each guest's restrictions in as much detail as possible.

Can I schedule or reschedule a reservation I have purchased?

All sales are final and non-refundable. Reservations cannot be cancelled or rescheduled. We recommend purchasing trabel insurance or securing travel and booking for separate days. Guests unable to keep their reservation may transfer the booking via their profile on Tock.

Do you charge for reservations in advance?

The full menu price is charged immediately upon booking a reservation. Beverages and any additional items ordered onsite will be charged at the time of purchase.

Do you accomodate larger groups?

We currently accept bookings for parties of up to 14 guests in our private dining rooms. For private events, a full restaurant buyout is possible for up to 50 guests. For all private event inquiries, please email us at events@mosukeparis.com
